Brief Biography

 

Jinyu Yu was awarded the Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Huazhong University of Science and Technologyin June, 2014. He is currently a PhD Candidate for Computer Science and Technology in Data Storage and Application Laboratory in Wuhan National Laboratory for Optoelectronics. And his supervisor is Professor Dan Feng and Assistant Professor Wei Tong. His current research interests include Fibre Channel over Ethernet(FCoE), I/O Virtualization,Virtual Machine Migration, and Software Defined Storage.
